Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lucky777 legal?▼
Lucky777 operates as an entertainment-based number prediction platform where participation is voluntary. We follow all applicable guidelines. Players must be 18+. We recommend checking your local state laws. Our draws are transparent and recorded.
How fast do I get my WhatsApp ticket?▼
Within 5 minutes of your payment being verified. You will receive your unique Ticket ID, game details, your picks, and draw time. Make sure your WhatsApp number is active and receiving messages.
What is UTR and where do I find it?▼
UTR (Unique Transaction Reference) is a 12-digit number generated after every UPI payment. You can find it in your GPay/PhonePe/Paytm payment history under "Transaction ID" or "Reference Number". Always enter this after paying so we can verify your entry.
What's the minimum and maximum entry?▼
Minimum entry is ₹5 for Jackpot and Prediction games, and ₹10 for Spin the Wheel. Maximum entry is ₹50 per ticket. You may purchase multiple tickets for the same draw. For USD players, minimum is $0.10 and maximum is $1.
How are draws conducted fairly?▼
Jackpot draws use a certified random number generator. Spin the Wheel is recorded on a real spinning wheel on live WhatsApp video. All prediction draws are announced live. Draw videos and screenshots are shared with all WhatsApp participants for full transparency.
How and when are prizes paid?▼
All prizes are paid directly to your UPI ID within 24 hours of the draw. For prize amounts above ₹10,000, we may request a PAN card photo for compliance. Small prizes (₹15–₹500) are typically paid within 2 hours.
Can I cancel my entry after paying?▼
Once a ticket is issued, entries cannot be cancelled or refunded. This is because the ticket is linked to a specific draw and registered in our system. If there is a technical issue with your entry, contact us immediately on WhatsApp.
Can I play from outside India?▼
Yes! International players can pay via Bank Transfer (SWIFT/wire) or crypto equivalent. Entry amounts are accepted in USD. Contact us on WhatsApp for international payment arrangements. Winners outside India receive prizes via international bank transfer.
